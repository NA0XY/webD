Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with FinanceIQ?

To get started, simply create an account by clicking "Sign Up". Once registered, you can access the dashboard to upload your financial data, use our calculators, and start managing your budget. We recommend starting with our Getting Started guide in the help section.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, your financial data is encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard security measures and never share your personal information with third parties. Your data is stored locally in your browser for budget and calculator features.

How do I upload my financial data?

You can upload your financial data in CSV format from the dashboard. Click on "Upload CSV" and select your file. The system supports various date formats and will automatically categorize your transactions. Check our data import guide for detailed instructions.

What calculators are available?

We offer comprehensive calculators including: Loan Calculator, Savings Calculator, Investment Calculator, Retirement Calculator, and Mortgage Calculator. Each calculator provides detailed breakdowns and visual charts to help you understand the results.

How do I set up a budget?

Go to the Budget page and start by adding budget categories (like Food, Transportation, Entertainment). Set monthly limits for each category, then track your expenses. The system will show your progress with visual indicators and alerts when you approach your limits.

Can I export my financial reports?

Yes, you can export your financial data and reports in various formats including PDF and CSV. Look for the "Export" buttons on the dashboard and budget pages. This allows you to keep records or share information with financial advisors.
